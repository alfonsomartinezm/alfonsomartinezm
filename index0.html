About UFC


The Ultimate Fighting Championship

The UFC was founded in 1993 and has since become the premier organization for mixed martial arts (MMA) competitions. It features the best fighters from around the world competing in various weight classes and styles. The UFC promotes events globally and is known for its thrilling fights and dynamic athletes.

The UFC has grown from a small, underground event to a global phenomenon, with millions of fans tuning in to watch the action. It has also played a crucial role in legitimizing MMA as a mainstream sport.

Top Fighters


Conor McGregor

Irish striker known for his knockout power and charisma.


Khabib Nurmagomedov

Russian grappler with an undefeated record.


Amanda Nunes

Brazilian champion in the women's bantamweight and featherweight divisions.
